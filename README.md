<h1>👨‍💻#EduBuddy</h1>

**A simple platform that helps students figure out what to learn, how to learn it, and where they stand.**
<br>
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
<h3>Features:</h3>

**1.📍 Career Roadmaps — clear step-by-step paths for different careers**

**2.🤖 AI Chatbot — ask doubts and get instant help.**

**3.Skill Quizzes — test your knowledge with different difficulty levels**

**4.📊 Self-Evaluation — understand your current skill level.**

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

<h3>🛠️ Tech Stack</h3>
Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
API: AI integration (chatbot support)
<br>
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

<h3>🧠Key Idea</h3>
**1.Students struggle because they don’t know what to learn next.**

<h3>This app gives:</h3>

**1.Direction (roadmaps)**
<br>

**2.Support (AI chatbot).**
<br>

**3.Feedback (quizzes).**
<hr>

<h3>Usage</h3>

**1.Open the app in your browser.**

**2.Choose a career path.**

**3.Follow the roadmap.**

**4.Use the chatbot to clear doubts.**

**5.Take quizzes to test your level.**
